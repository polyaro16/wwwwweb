Summer-autumn 1942
Two days ago, a law was issued, according to which all the grain is taken from the peasants and left at 10 kg per month per person. The city is completely disenfranchised, without any supplies, brutality and bullying on the part of the Germans. Heavily. There is no money at all. Yesterday, after work, I went to the market to sell things, but no one looked at anything. Mom's been hungry for two days. Voronezh, Lugansk, Voroshilovgrad were taken. Free trade is suspended from today..
Now I'm all alone in the whole apartment. Everyone's gone.There's no money, all you can think about is food and what to sell. In addition, there is constant unrest with Germany.I have a bad cold. The Germans have no victories now. They say our people have recaptured Voronezh! And other cities. In Stalingrad, there were battles for every house. They don't write anything now. I'm glad that at least my mom isn't freezing like I am. and I'll get over it somehow. The end of the war is not in sight.
