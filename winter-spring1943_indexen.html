Winter-spring 1943
The first day of the New Year, what does it bring with it? Where will I be?It is impossible to describe my agony! The room is +2 degrees, the stove is not burning, it is dark from smoke, dirty. Yesterday, having exhausted myself with the stove, I fell on the sofa and began to cry loudly.The Germans are in a bad way! They surrendered Voronezh, and the troops withdrew from the Caucasus. Heavy fighting at Stalingrad and on the Don. They say that Rostov has been handed over. The front is approaching Kiev. They reported on the radio that Kharkiv had been evacuated, that it had been turned into a pile of ruins and was no longer of any value. Poor city, what's going on there? Is the same fate awaiting us? It is terrible to think that all the streets will be turned into ruins. Hard days await Kiev. I spoke to the Kharkiv residents at the freight station. They told about the mockery of the Russians over the residents. Lots of arrests.
