Winter-autumn 1941 year
Terrible things are happening in Kiev, incessant explosions, rumbling, shooting, the sky is covered with clouds of smoke. The CHPP, depot and other structures were blown up. Everything of value in Kiev is destroyed. The windows ring incessantly. September 22 and 23 were calm. Only 24.09 explosions and fires began on Khreshchatyk. Every hour is news. In the middle of the day, a terrible order was posted for the Jews: tomorrow, 29.09, everyone should come to Lukyanovka at eight o'clock in the morning with documents and warm clothes. 8.12 the World War has begun. Japan went to war with the United States. Japan was joined by Germany and Italy. I wish the war would end soon! How many millions of people would sigh with joy! When will it end?! I wish the winter would pass and the warmth would come!
