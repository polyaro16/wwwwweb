Summer 1941 year
War. At four o'clock this morning, German planes raided Kiev. At seven o'clock, I heard bombs exploding in my sleep, but I thought it was just a normal study. In the morning, the city was anxious and tense, although no one knew anything. Many people thought that these were maneuvers.
At 12 o'clock, they announced that Molotov would be speaking on the radio. Everyone gathered in the dining room. Moscow, Dnepropetrovsk, Poltava, and Kharkiv are bombed every night. There are a lot of German paratroopers around Kiev and Bila Tserkva, they are being destroyed all the time. The guns roar incessantly. The Germans near Goloseyev. The Jews leave in a hurry. The stores are empty. Everyone is silent and gloomy. 28.08 left Dnepropetrovsk. 29.08 there was a rally in Kiev. The newspapers write that Kiev will not be handed over. If I am to die, I will die in Kiev.
