A witness to the events of the Great Patriotic War, a resident of Kiev Nina Gerasimova, left behind a diary with memories, which is now available on our website.
When reading the diary, you will mentally find yourself in June 1941, when children just finished school got to the front,without feeling their childhood at all...
In one moment life was divided into before and after...
Nina Nikolaevna Gerasimova
