Winter-spring 1944 year
Four days have passed, but more than a year has passed in these days. Today is a terrible day. They've been bombing since morning. I don't know where to go. I arrived in Kiev and went to the KZTS plant on 15.01, and was warmly welcomed. I'm currently working there as a chemical engineer. Now on vacation, from 2.02 to 18.02. Tomorrow I want to go to my mother to take some things and products. I was at the institute yesterday. I was accepted to the fourth year of the full-time Faculty of Chemistry. Now I'm on social insurance, 21.02 broke my left hand in the hand and now I have it in a cast. I visit the factory often, I help in any way I can. Finland asks for peace. Finally, at least one country has become smarter! We have victories! Uman is taken. A few days after Odessa was liberated. Lida did not feel the war, everything is engaged in amorous affairs. And I have all the difficulties, worries and no end in sight. 9.05 Sevastopol is taken. So far it is calm, there are no raids. They don't let you go from the factory, although there's nothing to do.
